Agile Assisted Acquisitions from 18F

"We'll be your trusted technical brains for Feds acquiring software using modern software techniques."

Agile Assisted Acquisitions is a project of 18F, a technology incubator within the General Services Administrator.

The purpose of Agile Assisted Acquisition is to provide a knowledgeable aide-de-camp to government program managers and other leaders who want assistance in designing and managing acquisitions of software performed by outside firms. We offer a variety of services at cost-recovery prices from knowledgable and experienced software engineers.

Unlike other repositories of 18F, this repo does not invite contributions from the public. However, all materials found here may be freely redistributed under the Creative Commons license.

Current Documents

  • Agile Assisted Acquistions This is a .PDF of our deck we use for in-person presentations. It is not meant to be read as a stand-alone document.
  • How to Run Your Own 3-Sprint Agile Workshop This is a short (10-page) how-to guide on running a one-afternoon Agile Workshop as an exercise. We provide coaching for such exercises.
